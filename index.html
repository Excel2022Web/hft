About

A spark. A small wish to change the world. That’s all it takes to start building a product which might be the next big thing.

Are you a beginner dabbling with ‘Hello World’ lines of code, looking to delve deep into the realm of 0s and 1s Or are you looking to use your tech prowess to build something for society?

Excel and KeyValue presents HackForTomorrow (HFT), a 24 hour-long hackathon, which seeks to promote the creation of novel and distinctive technical solutions to societal problems. The hackathon, held as part of Excel 2022, the techno-managerial symposium of Govt. Model Engineering College has attracted over 300 participants and resulted in some incredible inventions.

Why participate

The top 3 teams of HFT get a direct entry into the finals of IBeTo 2021. Check out the website of IBeTo here.

Get your hands on thrilling cash rewards from an INR 1,50,000 prize pool.

Swags, Stickers, Redbull, Munchies….

Join forces and network with some of the nation's top thinkers.

Attend speaker sessions led by seasoned developers from prominent companies.
